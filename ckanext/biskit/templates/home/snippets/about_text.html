About BISKIT

The project “Blood Information System for Crisis Intervention and Management (BISKIT)” aims to contribute to the resilience of the South African blood supply system in times of crises. By exploiting the advantages of digital technology, the project is going to;

1. develop a demonstrator of a data-based information system (including a user interface) enabling practitioners working in blood establishments to make informed decisions concerning the distribution and transport of blood and blood products during and after a crisis.
2. prepare crisis management plans for blood establishments (including crisis communication strategies) which take the need for cooperation between different government layers, hospitals, transfusion centres, transport companies etc. into account.
3. design training concepts to build up crisis management capacities among local staff based on the “train-the-trainer”-approach.
